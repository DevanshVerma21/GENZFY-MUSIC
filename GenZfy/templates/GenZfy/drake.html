DRAKE GRAHAM

Biography

Drake (Aubrey Drake Graham) was born on October 24, 1986, in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. He is one of the most influential and successful artists of the 21st century, known for his contributions to rap, R&B, and pop music. With a career that spans over a decade, Drake has become a global icon, known for his versatility, emotional depth, and ability to blend genres.

Drake initially gained fame as an actor on the Canadian teen drama series Degrassi: The Next Generation, where he played Jimmy Brooks. However, it was his foray into music that truly catapulted him to worldwide stardom. He released his first mixtape, "So Far Gone" in 2009, which included the hit single "Best I Ever Had" and earned him widespread recognition in the hip-hop world.

Some of the popular tracks of Drake are "God's plan","First person shooter","Laugh now cry later".

Drake has also broken numerous records in the music industry, including becoming the first artist to have over 50 billion streams on Spotify. He is a multi-time Grammy Award winner and has topped the charts in multiple countries. In addition to his music career, Drake is also a successful entrepreneur with his own record label, OVO Sound, and is known for his philanthropy, having donated millions to various causes.
